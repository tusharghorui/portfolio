Microcontroller based smart home automation

The system assembled here is a fairly simple system. We are using two ultrasonic sensors here in order to detect people going in and out of the room. Thus, when the room is empty, the lights and other appliances will be switched off in the room. The PIR sensor is present to detect any movements in the room. These values from the sensors will be sent to the ESP32 microcontroller which will act as the server here. The ESP32 will host a website which will be available at our devices.

Smart irrigation system with remote monitoring facilities

Smart irrigation systems offer a variety of advantages over traditional irrigation systems. Smart irrigation systems can optimize water levels based on things such as soil moisture and weather predictions. This is done with wireless moisture sensors that communicate with the smart irrigation controls and help inform the system whether or not the landscape is in need of water.
